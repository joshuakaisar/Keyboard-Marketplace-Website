Bespoke Commission

Starting at $1800

  • Our team will work the client to come up with a design to the client's liking.
  • Our team gets the custom-designed keyboard manufactured and helps source additions components.
  • Cost of manufacturing/sourcing is to be paid upfront by the client.
  • The keyboard assembly will be recorded for the client.
  • Invoice sent for build rate plus return shipping.
  • Assembled keyboard will be shipped back to the client after completion.

Build Service
